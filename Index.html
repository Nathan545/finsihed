PENTESTING HUB

Penetration testing, more commonly called pentesting,
is the practice of finding holes that could be exploited in an application,
network or system with the goal of detecting security vulnerabilities that a hacker
could use against it. Pentesting is used to detect three things: how the system reacts to an attack,
which weak spots exist that could be breached, if any, and what data could be stolen from an active system.
